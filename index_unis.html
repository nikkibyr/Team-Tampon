University Campaigns

Many universities across the United Kingdom have committed to offering free sustainable period products to anyone who may need them.

Either through Student Unions, government funded action, or the institutions themselves, many universities are leading the charge in tackling period poverty among their student body. Some examples of successful campaigns can be seen in York, Worcester, Nottingham, Leeds, Stirling, Edinburgh, and UCL.
