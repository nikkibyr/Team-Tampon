England

Authorities in England have agreed that menstrual hygiene is fundamental to providing dignified care.

In January 2019, NHS England committed to providing free sanitary products to women and girls in hospitals. Local authorities across England have started to offer free sanitary products for staff and users and by the end of 2020 all students at schools, colleges, and universities should have access to free products. Local authorities are receiving funding to help tackle period poverty in areas where levels of deprivation are highest, whilst further funding is being invested to improve facilities and equipment in schools and to ensure access to good sanitary facilities for all children and young people who need them.

Source: https://www.rcn.org.uk
